--- title: 'JazzUI - Not your average prototypes' slug: jazzui-not-your-average-prototypes created: !!timestamp '2013-11-27 23:37:58' extends: blog-rst.j2 status: public tags: - nodejs - jazzui - prototyping - project --- {% mark excerpt %} Today I released version 1.0 of a new Web prototyping tool, `JazzUI `_, which, like many of my projects, I built because I couldn't find the right tool already in existence. I wanted jade, angular, and lesscss, live updating, automatic fixture data generation, component(1) support, and easy export of real usable code. So that's what I made. {% endmark %} For me, I have always been frustrated when prototyping tools fall so far short of the power and flexibility of working with real code. CSS allows you to theme a whole site with a minimum of changes and javascript allows you to generated fixture data and have rich interaction. Add on top of that angularjs and lesscss, and there's a lot you can do with very little code.
