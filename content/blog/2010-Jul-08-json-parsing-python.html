How is this done? `Codetalker `_ **Codetalker** is a parsing library that I've been developing on and off for the past few months, which aims to facilitate easy and intuitive declaration of grammars, in a pythonic fashion that is influenced by the tradition of BNF, without compromising speed or flexibility.
